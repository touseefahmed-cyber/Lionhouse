Supernutrients B2B Ecommerce Website

As pioneering early adopters of the emerging superfood trend, Supernutrients have spent 16+ years establishing themselves as wholesale market leaders.

As pioneering early adopters of the emerging superfood trend, Supernutrients have spent 16+ years establishing themselves as wholesale market leaders. After a period of change within the business, we were thrilled to be commissioned to help them create an entirely new online home.

They required a website which not only reinforced their reputation as a respected authority within the industry, but also improved and supported their existing B2B customer’s shopping experience and strategically targeted and attracted new customers alike.

Working closely with their team and utilising our trusted discovery process, we were able to plan, prototype, design and deliver a custom ecommerce Shopify solution which surpassed their expectations.

We improved product categorisation, focussed their presentation and created a useful account system which supported customers at every step of their buying journey.
