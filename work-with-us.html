Work with us

Calling all designers, creatives, animators, project managers, developers, strategists, whizz-kids, tech experts… Let’s talk careers@lionbhouse.com

We are purposefully small, but an incredibly passionate team who work with an extended network of outstanding creatives, developers and strategists across the world.

We’ve built our team with the sole purpose of providing positive change for our clients, as such we are always looking for the best talent to help us do this.

Talent no knows no bounds and believe a diverse, equitable and inclusive agency delivers more creative and engaging results. We just want to work with good people, we want to hear from anyone whatever your background, experience, skills and outlook.

It doesn’t matter if you’re experienced or taking your first steps, we want to work with people who are passioanate, keen and eager to do great work. We’ll provide great opportunities to thrive.

With remote working and studio based opportunities, location isn’t important. You’ll get to work with selection amazing consumer and B2B brands, some household names and some challengers. See our list of services to see the breadth of skills we offer.

Designers

  • all levels
  • remote & hybrid working
  • freelance & full/part time opportunities

About the role

Influencing the design of a global brand website, finely crafting the development of a self-service digital product, helping optimize an e-commerce experience, thinking about how to get the most out of educational content, designing sales support tools to help our B2B Clients sell better or a consumer app that makes life just that little bit easier.

This position will give you the great opportunity to learn, growing your career from the close guidance of a leading team of digital specialists.

Responsibilities

Discover – Understanding the right challenges to be solved - by asking the right questions, analysing user behaviour, talking to customers, understanding Client business objectives, best practices and reviewing competitor websites or apps. From this you will identify key user insights and develop hypotheses to design effective experiences.

Define – Looking at how existing content, features and processes work together. Using techniques like content audits, facilitated workshops and structured brainstorms to collaboratively develop useful frameworks like sitemaps and user journeys.

Design – Articulate your design-thinking using wireframes and interactive prototypes. Our digital work is varied - so you could be working on anything from responsive websites to native apps, interactive content to marketing campaigns.
